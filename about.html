About CRE Tool Hub

CRE Tool Hub provides free commercial real estate calculators designed for property investors, analysts, and real estate professionals. Our suite of professional-grade calculation tools helps you evaluate investment opportunities, analyze property performance, and make data-driven decisions with confidence.

Every calculator on CRE Tool Hub runs entirely in your browser using client-side technology. We never collect, store, or transmit your property data to any server. Your financial information and investment analysis remain completely private. This privacy-first approach ensures that sensitive deal information, proprietary calculations, and confidential property data stay secure on your device.

Our commercial real estate calculator suite includes essential metrics for comprehensive property analysis: Cap Rate Calculator for capitalization rate and return evaluation, NOI Builder for net operating income calculations, Cash-on-Cash Return Calculator for measuring actual investment returns, DSCR Calculator for debt service coverage ratio and lender requirements, Loan-to-Value (LTV) Calculator for financing analysis, Debt Yield Calculator for commercial underwriting standards, and Breakeven Occupancy Calculator for multifamily property evaluation.

CRE Tool Hub was created by commercial real estate professionals to address the need for accurate, accessible, and privacy-focused calculation tools. Whether you're analyzing your first multifamily property, evaluating office building cap rates, calculating debt service coverage for commercial loans, or performing comprehensive investment analysis across multiple property types, our free calculators provide the professional-grade formulas and instant results you need.

All calculations use industry-standard formulas trusted by institutional investors, commercial lenders, and real estate professionals. Share your results via URL with partners, lenders, or team members, or download PDF reports for your records. Every tool is designed for speed, accuracy, and ease of use—whether you're conducting quick property screenings or detailed underwriting analysis.

CRE Tool Hub is completely free to use with no registration, no subscription fees, and no hidden costs. We believe professional real estate analysis tools should be accessible to everyone—from first-time commercial investors to seasoned portfolio managers.
